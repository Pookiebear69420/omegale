Welcome to ChatConnect

ChatConnect lets you chat with random strangers online. Please read and accept the rules before continuing.

WARNING: You may encounter nudity, sexual content, or other inappropriate behavior. Use at your own risk.

Rules and Guidelines

  1. You must be 18 years or older to use this service.
  2. Nudity and sexually explicit conduct are prohibited in the video chat section.
  3. Do not broadcast copyrighted content or music.
  4. Be respectful. No harassment, threats, or bullying.
  5. Do not share personal information such as your full name, address, or phone number.
  6. Recording or screenshotting chats without consent may be illegal in your area.
  7. Report any illegal activity to the appropriate authorities.
  8. We do not monitor chats but may record and report illegal activity.
